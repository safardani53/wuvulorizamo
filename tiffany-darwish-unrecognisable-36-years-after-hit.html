AN eighties pop superstar looks unrecognisable as she turns 52 after her massive hit and even posing nude for Playboy.

Tiffany Darwish shot to fame when she sang I Think We're Alone Now - a cover from Tommy James and the Shondells classic.

The star, who is now 52, is also just known as Tiffany, released the track on her self-titled debut Album.

Soon after she released more chart-topping singles such as Could've Been and I Saw Him Standing There.

In total Tiffany has released a whopping 11 studio albums with the star still performing.

Tiffany brought out her latest album in 2018 – Pieces of Me.

She has since sold over 15 million albums to date and set a record as the youngest female artist to top the US Billboard charts with her debut album.

She also tried her hand at acting and made a cameo in the hit TV sitcom How I Met Your Mother.

In 2002 Tiffany bared all when she stripped off for Playboy magazine, leaving her fans hot under the collar.

She even had a catfight with Debbie Gibsonin in the movie Mega Python vs Gateroid.
