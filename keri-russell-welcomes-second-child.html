Keri Russell Welcomes Second Child!

May 15, 2024
blog

Keri Russell’s Christmas present arrived a little late:

A baby girl born December 27!

The Felicity actress, 35, gave birth to daughter Willa Lou Deary. It’s the second child for Russell and husband Shane Deary, parents to son River Russell Deary, 4.

The beautiful actress met Deary, a carpenter, through a mutual friend, and they wed on Valentine’s Day 2007. The growing family lives in Brooklyn, N.Y.

Summing up the event, “I just feel like child birth is one of the most powerful things that happens to a woman,” Russell told the New York Observer.

The actress next stars in the romantic comedy Austenland, out this year.

Congratulations to the whole family!
