KELLY Clarkson put her best foot forward and showed off her “flawless” voice with a cover of Nancy Sinatra’s 1960s hit, These Boots Are Made For Walkin’, on Wednesday.

The rendition of the scorned woman song comes a few months after the 38-year-old singer filed for divorce from husband of seven years Brandon Blackstock in June.

The mother of two, who shares daughter River Rose, 6, and son Remington, 4, with Brandon, tackled the classic song in a new edition of The Kelly Clarkson Show’s Kellyoke series.

Kelly emotionally sings out: “These boots are made for walking, And that's just what they'll do. 

“One of these days these boots are gonna walk all over you.”

Fans took to the comments of the video to share how much they can “feel” what she’s crooning, which comes amid her divorce from her husband and the father of her children.

One happy listener wrote: “This has got to be one the most HONEST versions of this song.”

Another said: “The goosebumps gal!”

A third noted: “Gurl. I would not want to be her ex right now. Slay, Kelly. SLAY!”

While others added on, “Scornfully enticing lil’numba” and “Keeping walking Queen, we gotcha back!”

Just a day earlier, the Voice coach continued to show her confident side claiming Right Said Fred’s I’m Too Sexy as her “theme song” for the day.

She’s been all about standing her ground lately, often slamming trolls commenting on her divorce, with some recently blaming her “work ethic” for the demise of her marriage after she filled in for Simon Cowell on America’s Got Talent.

The singer tweeted on Thursday: "Wow. Shaming a woman who has a great work ethic, is a great mom, and who steps up and fills in when a friends asks for a favor because that’s actually what 'good old country girls' do.... this can’t be who you are deep down. I have more faith in your heart. Aim higher please."

During the episode, the singer was slammed as "dumb" on social media alongside hosts Heidi Klum, Sofia Vergara and Howie Mandel for cutting ukulele player Feng E.

Kelly appeared on the show after Simon broke three vertebrae, missing his spinal cord by a centimeter, in a horrendous fall from an electric bike.

The American Idol alum announced her divorce from husband Brandon in June after seven years of marriage.

The Sun previously confirmed that the courts had approved a privately compensated judge, Judge Scott M. Gordon, for Kelly and Brandon's divorce proceedings until December 2021.
