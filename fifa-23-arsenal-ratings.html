What does this mean for Arsenal players?

The biggest signing for Arsenal this year is Gabriel Jesus, and when you look at his player rating it’s clear to see why.

83 is a solid score for an Arsenal player, sitting just behind midfielders Partey and Ødegaard, who rate at 84 a piece.

Speaking of Ødegaard, he’s now Arsenal’s captain and has gone up one rating point to show his improvements. 

Goalkeeper Ramsdale has also gained a point alongside Tomiyasu and Nketiah.
