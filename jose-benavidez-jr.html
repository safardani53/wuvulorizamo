Who is Jose Benavidez Jr.:

Jose Benavidez Jr. is a famous Boxer. He was born on May 15, 1992 and his birthplace is Los Angeles, CA. Jose is also well known as, Mexican-American professional boxer who has fought in both the welterweight and light welterweight classes. He had an impressive 120-5 record as an amateur and won his first 27 pro bouts before losing to Terence Crawford in 2018.

Jose is originated from United States. He reached the title fight at the 2009 USA Boxing National Championship but lost to Frankie Gomez.
