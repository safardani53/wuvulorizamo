Bruce Springsteen 2023: How to get tickets to Dublin shows

September 15, 2024
blog

Tickets for Bruce Springsteen's UK tour dates next year are available to buy now.

Keen to see the Boss perform? Read on to find out where you can get tickets.

Tickets for these much-hyped gigs are available to buy from Ticketmaster - we've got UK dates and locations listed below.

Tickets for these two dates go live on the Ticketmaster website at 8am on Friday 24 May.

The legendary rock and roll maestro will embark on a huge European tour in 2023, and there are a number of UK cities confirmed in the schedule - but tickets are selling fast.

Springsteen and his long-serving outfit the E Street Band have announced a number of 2023 dates at major revenues across a number of European countries.

They will be playing to crowds in France, Spain, Germany, Austria, Switzerland and Scandinavia in what promises to be an absolute smash of a world tour.

Read on for the full list of tour dates - and keep in mind: there are likely to be plenty of additions in the months to come.

If and when the UK dates are pencilled in (and we really think it's a case of when) we'll be updating this page - so make sure you bookmark this article.
