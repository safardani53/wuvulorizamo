THE SINGER is embarking on a tour that will visit a host of cities.

Kim Petras took to social media to announce her upcoming 2023 tour.

What cities is the 'Feed the Beast' tour coming to?

On Wednesday, June 21, 2023, Petras announced her 2023 tour via Twitter.

The German singer-songwriter also shared the news on Instagram.

How can I get tickets to the Kim Petras tour?

USA Today reported that pre-sales for the 'Feed the Beast' tour will begin on Thursday, June 22, 2023.

On June 21, Petras tweeted: "text me at 949-331-0609 for access to an exclusive presale tomorrow" followed by the hand heart emoji.

As Petras shared on social media, the general sale will start on Monday, June 26, 2023, with tickets becoming available at 10am local time.

According to the 30-year-old star's website, tickets can be purchased via Ticketmaster.

There are also VIP packages available.

When is Kim Petras releasing her album?

Petras is releasing her Feed the Beast album on Friday, June 23, 2023.

On Tuesday, May 23, 2023, she took to social media to announce her upcoming project.
