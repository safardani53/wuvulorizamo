Australian tennis player Max Purcell girlfriend, Chloe Deith, is an educator and shop assistant. The duo have been together since mid-2021. 

After their relationship became official, Max and Chloe haven’t back from expressing their love for one another.  

Born on 2 April 1998, Max Purcell is an Australian professional tennis player. The athlete has achieved tremendous success, ranking as high as No. 67 per the ATP.

Purcell turned pro in 2016 and established himself as the best-emerging talent.

At the 2020 Tokyo Olympics, Purcell defeated world No. 15 Felix Auger-Aliassime in a straight set to earn his best career victory. 

He reached the final at the 2020 Australian Open alongside Luke Saville, where they lost to Rajeev Ram and Brit Joe Salisbury. Moreover, Purcell paired with Dayana at the 2021 US Open and reached the quarterfinals.  

At the 2022 Wimbledon Championship in doubles, he won his first-ever Grand Slam title with Matthew Edden. Additionally, the duo even came second at the 2022 Australian Open.

Throughout his career, Purcell has secured three ATP doubles titles. In 2023, he decided to focus on his singles career.

Max Purcell Girlfriend: Chloe Deith

The Australian athlete caused a stir among fans when he appeared with his girlfriend, Chloe Deith, an educator and shop assistant.

Although they are yet to open about their first official meeting, the two seem together since 2021. The couple went Instagram official about their relationship in April 2021. They frequently post pictures of each other on their handles.   

Chloe resides in Sydney, Australia, and graduated from Asquith Girls High School.

According to her LinkedIn, Chloe, presently works as a shop assistant at Bausele and as an educator at Catholic Care.

Apart from her working career, she engages herself in motocross racing. Her Instagram profile gives the impression that she enjoys traveling.

Nevertheless, she frequently attends all his games and is the biggest encourager of her boyfriend.
