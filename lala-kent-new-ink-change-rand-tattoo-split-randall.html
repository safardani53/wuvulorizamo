Lala Kent gets new ink to change 'Rand' tattoo after split from fiance Randall Emmett amid cheating

July 04, 2024
blog

LALA Kent is putting another part of her failed engagement behind her, changing a tattoo of Randall Emmett's name into a new power mantra.

The Vanderpump Rules star had the tattoo, which states "Rand" turned into one that now reads "bRand New."

The new art can be seen in photos for her new eyewear collection that Lala shared on Instagram.

Lala and Randall officially ended their three-year relationship in October, shortly after allegations that Randall, 50, cheated on Lala, 31, while away on a trip filming in Tennessee.

Photos of Randall and two unidentified women entering a hotel together made their way online.

LALA GOES 'NUCLEAR'

The Vanderpump Rules star reportedly went "nuclear" on her fiancé when she began getting "DMs about him hooking up with other girls," a source revealed to The Sun days after the split.

"She had always put up with him having this whole other life away traveling and doing business but she'd had enough. 

"Getting those DMs I think was humiliating for her so she cut things off, but Randall didn't think for a second Lala was really leaving him," they explained.

The insider continued: "She'd done this before where she lashed out, deleted him off social media, gets mad. But she always comes back.

"To him, she was taking a breather and he was giving her some space. No one in the VPR cast thought she called off her engagement for real."

Lala began reshaping her life not long after.

In early November, she removed Randall's name from their podcast.

She opened a recent episode of the podcast sharing that her friend and assistant would be on the show instead of her ex. 

CLEARING THE AIR

Before they welcomed their guest, Brittany Cartwright, Lala wanted to clear the air with all of her listeners.

The mother-of-one said: “I’m excited for today’s podcast, and I obviously want to acknowledge that I’m sure everyone knows what is going on between Randall and myself and just in my personal life. 

“There will be a time I feel comfortable enough to open up and share, today is definitely not that day.

“…In this moment, it’s not about me, it’s about my kid, and privacy is the only thing I’m looking for, for her sake.”

After a big, emotional sigh, the Bravo star continued: “It’s been a lot, and I’m going through a lot, and I just at this point in time want to protect my child. She’s my number one priority.

Lala and Randall got engaged in 2018 and welcomed daughter Ocean in March this year.

They first met in 2015 when Randall got his assistant to approach Lala about appearing in a film.
