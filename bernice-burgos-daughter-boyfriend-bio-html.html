Bernice Burgos Bio: Dating History, Daughters and Plastic Surgery Addressed

American fashion enthusiast Bernice Burgos has been in the entertainment industry for quite a while now. 

She rose to prominence for featuring on music videos for American rappers like Rick Ross and J-Cole. She has also featured on MTV's comedy show, Wild 'N Out.

Here's everything that you need to know about the model, including her bio.

Bernice Burgos Before Plastic Surgery

Burgos was raised in the tri-state area between New Jersey and New York City. 

She possesses an Afro-American ethnicity and holds American nationality.

Born on 17 April 1980, Burgos is at the age of 41 as of this time of writing. Her height is five 5 feet and seven and a half inches (1.75 m.)

Despite being in her early-40s, Burgos doesn't look so, thanks to plastic surgery. And she has been pretty vocal about the beauty enhancements throughout her career. 

Bernice Burgos before and after plastic surgery. 

On 26 July 2017, Burgos answered the burning questions regarding her plastic surgery in an interview on The Breakfast Club (via Hollywood Life.)

She confessed she had plastic surgery and admitted not one but multiple procedures. "I’ve done a bit of everything," she stated. 

As per her accounts, she first went under the knife for her butt enhancement to "fill the two dents." And the surgery was far from legal and safe. 

They did the plastic surgery in a basement. Thankfully, the procedure healed and gave her the desired shape. 

Besides plastic surgery, Burgos also workout timely to stay fit and maintain her beauty.

Bernice Burgos's Dating History

Burgos has had an eventful dating life. In addition, she has been rumored to be romantically linked with multiple A-listers. 

She was reportedly in a relationship with an American businessman, Marion 'Suge' Knight, in early 2010. 

In 2015, she was thought to be dating Drake. The rumors of their relationship spurred after Drake allegedly gifted her a Bentley. However, the rumors amounted to nothing. 

Instead, she was speculated to be dating rapper Clifford Joseph Harris Jr., aka T.I., in 2016. Rumors were rife that Burgos was why T.I. divorced his then wife, Tameka Cottle (Tiny).

While Burgos never confirmed dating T.I, she clapped back at haters who were blaming her for T.I's divorce. 

"Always been a home wrecker? That home BEEN broke. And I never dealt with a married man in my life so please STOP trying to declass my character and my name!" she told a troll.

She also highlighted that divorce meant the two former spouses were moving on in their lives, and their marriage had failed. She iterated she had nothing to do with the split. 

Was Bernice Burgos Pregnant with Migos Quavo's Baby?

Burgos’ dating life again made headlines when she was reported to be in a relationship with hip-hop artist Migos Quavo.

They were thought to be dating one another in January 2018 after the pair had been spotted out together by a random fan.

Shortly after they were spotted together, Hollywood Life magazine added more to the fuel. It revealed that American rapper Cardi B told an insider that she was rooting for the possible boyfriend-girlfriend duo.

When Cardi found out Quavo was spending time with Burgos, she thought they were perfect for each other. "Quavo is like a brother to Cardi and she is all about love," the insider added. 

Not only that, just weeks after they had supposedly started seeing each other, Burgos was also reported to be pregnant with the artist’s first child.

However, the rumors were nothing more than mere speculation. Nothing more on their dating or Burgos's pregnancy ever surfaced. 

Is Bernice Burgos Married? 

Burgos's marital status has been a matter of curiosity for her fans and followers, given that the 41-year-old model is a mother of two daughters: Ashley Burgos and Sarai Burgos.

But the mom-of-two has never disclosed the identity of the father of her kids. She has also never affirmed being married to anyone or having a husband. 

Having said that, it is known that she gave birth to her first daughter Ashley when she was at the age of 15.

Her boyfriend wasn't a fan of her pregnancy, and Burgos had to raise her daughter as a single teenage mother. 

Bernice Burgos with her daughter Ashley Burgos in September 2021. (Photo: Instagram)

"Having a child at the age of 15, I had to like rush into being a mother…." Burgos told The Breakfast Club. She recounted she was embarrassed being pregnant since she looked way younger than her age. 

"People were like, ‘Oh my God! A baby having a baby.’… It just felt like, I don’t know, it didn’t feel good," she recalled, adding she had to act like 30 even when she was just 15. 

Around ten years later, Burgos gave birth to her second daughter. However, she has strictly kept her kids away from the limelight.

That being said, it is known that Burgos's eldest daughter has already given birth to a baby. 

Ashley was pregnant with her first child in 2017 and gave birth in March 2018.

As a proud grandmother, Burgos handled the situation pretty well. She slammed the hecklers criticizing her daughter’s early pregnancy and thanked those congratulating the news.

Bernice Burgos’ Net Worth

Before she entered the entertainment industry, Burgos used to work as a bartender in the Bronx. It was while she was bartending she was discovered. 

As a result, Burgos spearheaded her modeling career and starred in multiple music videos. She also earned a huge paycheck as a featured artist on musician DJ Khaled's music video, 'Do You Mind' in 2016. 

Besides her modeling, a major source of her net worth is her clothing line, Bold and Beautiful, which she started with her daughter. The clothing line focuses on women’s clothes.
