Kari Ann Peniche Leaves Rehab After 3 Day Stint

May 31, 2024
blog

Celebrity Rehab star and former beauty queen Kari Ann Peniche has left an undisclosed rehab facility after only three days of treatment. This is not the first time the ex-Playboy model has bailed early.

This time around Kari Ann was being treated at a high-end treatment facility for her addiction to meth. At the time of check-in her boyfriend led her to believe that he would follow suit and check-in at the facility. He was still a no-show after three days so she left to go and find him.

TMZ is reporting that Peniche has located her boyfriend and they are working on their relationship. She does plan to return to the facility at some point…

Kari Ann appeared on the first season of Sex Rehab with Dr. Drew, the third season of Celebrity Rehab and there was a brief stint on the second season of Sober House. She apparently realizes she needs help but just can’t seem to finish what she starts.

Last August Kari made headlines when a 12-minute sex tape leaked, showing her escapades with actor Eric Dane and his wife Rebecca Gayheart.
