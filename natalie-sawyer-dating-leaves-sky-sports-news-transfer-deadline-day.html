Who is Natalie Sawyer dating and what club does former Sky Sports News host support?

NATALIE SAWYER will not be returning to our screens next transfer deadline day.

The Sky Sports News presenter was a regular alongside Jim White before leaving to pursue other ventures.

Here is everything you need to know about her.

Who is Natalie Sawyer?

Natalie Sawyer was born in Ealing in 1979 to an English father and Bulgarian mother.

She was previously married to Sky Sports News colleague Sam Matterface, and together they have a son called Sawyer who was born in 2012.

The pair divorced in 2014.

What football team does Natalie Sawyer support?

Natalie Sawyer is a lifelong Brentford fan and still attends most home matches.

She has previously appeared on public buses as part of a Bees' campaign to sell tickets.

Who is Natalie Sawyer dating now?

Since 2015, Sawyer is believed to have been dating footballer Jonathan Douglas, who left Ipswich earlier in the summer.

Back then, she posted a photo on Instagram of the pair in Iceland together.

Douglas previously spent four years playing at her favourite club, Brentford, before moving to Suffolk in 2015.
