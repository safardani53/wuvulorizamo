Mindy Kalings Loungewear Set Is an Easy Fall Uniform

August 11, 2024
blog

Mindy Kaling is just like the rest of us: She’s excited when she gets a new package, she cracks jokes about herself, and she wears loungewear. 

Late last week, the Never Have I Ever co-creator, 44, was hyped after receiving Issa Rae’s prosecco line Viarae and shared her package on her Instagram stories, where she teased that the sparkling wine and glasses were “for me and my lover… or me and my assistant more like.” 

As the multiple videos played out, we were also able to piece together what she was wearing. In one, we caught a glimpse of her brown pants, and in another, where she was talking directly to the camera, it was obvious that she was wearing a brown sweatshirt. 

While we can’t be certain that Kaling’s outfit was a set, it sure looks like it. And it looked comfortable and put-together, which are two of the most important factors when it comes to clothing. (It also reminded us of another time the star has worn a matching set.) And it can be thrown on in mere seconds and is blissfully easy to style.

We found eight sets for under $50 that look like Kaling’s — and they’re all available at Amazon.
