Sodastream launches new Art Sparkling Water Maker to curb your fizzy drink habit

August 05, 2024
blog

SODASTREAM have launched a new machine, the Art Sparkling Water Maker.

A more sustainable way of fulfilling your fizzy drink cravings - we can't wait to get our hands on one.

  • Sodasteram Art Sparkling Water Maker, £139.99, John Lewis - buy here

SodaStream has announced the launch of its most stylish and innovative machine to date, the SodaStream Art.

Available to shop at John Lewis, Argos, Amazon, Robert Dyas as well as sodastream.co.uk.

It's priced at £139.99 and is on sale now and comes in chic black and classic white.

It also comes a Fuse bottle and one Quick Connect Co2 cylinder, with one one gas cylinder making up to 60 litres of bubbles.

However you can also recycle your gas refills to save £10 on the cost of a new one, exchanging your gas cylinders at retailers across the country. 

Shoppers can also pick up a Summer Limited edition Kit on Sodastream's website for £149.99 which comes with a Quick Connect Co2 cylinder, a Fuse Bottle, and six flavours including 7UP and Pepsi Max.

But what makes the new bottle so special?

The new Art machine features a "slim silhouette, chic design, stainless steel trim and a unique carbonation lever" which users now have the chance to create a "drink that perfectly suits their tastes, experimenting with levels of fizz and different flavour combinations".

It uses a one click connection for Quick Connect Cylinder so it's easier than ever to use and for the first time, the new machine will come with a dishwasher safe bottle.

The reusable bottle will allow fizzy fans to enjoy sparkling water again and again, whilst saving thousands of single-use plastic water bottles from being used.
