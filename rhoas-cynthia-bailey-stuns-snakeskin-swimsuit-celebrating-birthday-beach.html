RHOA's Cynthia Bailey stuns in snakeskin swimsuit while celebrating 54th birthday on the beach

REAL Housewives of Atlanta star Cynthia Bailey stunned in a snakeskin swimsuit.

The Bravo star flaunted her curves while celebrating her 54th birthday on the beach.

Cynthia took to Instagram on Monday to share three photos of herself standing in the water as she rocked a snakeskin swimsuit that featured a low cut front.

The RHOA star played with her hair as she smiled off into the distance in the first and third photos, while she donned a more serious expression in the second snap.

Alongside the photos, Cynthia wrote: “It’s #nationalbikiniday & i know that this is a swimsuit and not a bikini. however, i love these pictures from my 54th birthday trip to Jamacia.

“So let me be great and join in on the fun!

“Thanks again @noellerobinson & @idle.awhile for making my day so special!🌞”

The caption concluded with the hashtags #mondaymonday #idleawhile and #54cynt.

The photos were from a trip Cynthia took to celebrate her birthday on February 19.

Cynthia has starred on RHOA since the third season, though she recently hinted that she may not be back for season 14.

Rumors began to swirl that Cynthia was leaving the Bravo show when she removed the RHOA hashtag from her Instagram bio.

However, the TV personality insisted she hasn’t been fired.

She told Love B. Scott that she chose to remove the hashtag from her bio a while back, adding that she hasn’t been fired “to her knowledge.”

However, Cynthia implied that she has not yet been asked back for the next season of RHOA as she said she “would like the option to return.”

RHOA is not her only involvement in the franchise, as is set to star on the upcoming series Real Housewives All Stars.

The show will feature seven cast members from the Real Housewives franchise, including Cynthia, RHONJ stars Teresa Giudice, 49, and Melissa Gorga, 42, New York socialite Ramona Singer, 64, fellow RHONY star Luann de Lesseps, 56, and RHOA star Kenya Moore, 50.

Back in May, Cynthia’s daughter Noelle Robinson revealed that her brief stint on RHOA got her fired from a waitressing job.

While appearing on the first-ever Housewives Kids special on Watch What Happens Live with Andy Cohen, the 21-year-old said of the show: “It definitely made my experience a lot different and harder for me socially. 

“People assume you’re bougie or stuck up and that’s just really not true. I definitely think it made it harder for me at college.
