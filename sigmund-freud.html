Who is Sigmund Freud:

Sigmund Freud is a famous Psychiatrist. He was born on May 6, 1856 and his birthplace is Pribor, Czech Republic. Sigmund is also well known as, Father of Modern Psychiatry who was the first to come up with psychiatric concepts such as the unconscious, the Ego, and the Oedipal Complex. He was known for analyzing his patient’s dreams and he theorized that dreams themselves were forms of wish-fulfillment.

Sigmund is originated from Czech Republic. He founded psychoanalysis and inspired other psychologists like B.F. Skinner.
