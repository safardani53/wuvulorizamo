LEICESTER CITY hero Jamie Vardy and wife Rebekah have received some banter online for their glamorous fancy dress outfits.

The married couple have been trolled and praised by fellow footballers and fans after the 34-year-old striker posted a pic on Instagram.

Vardy uploaded a picture of him and Rebekah in fancy dress, although it's not obvious who the pair were supposed to be.

In what can only be described as questionable seventies style, the ex-England forward wore brown leather flares and a blonde wig.

He's also donned tinted spectacles and a groovy dark brown shirt, showing the peace sign to the camera.

Wife Rebekah wore similar glasses, a brown wig and a glittering one-piece outfit, fitted with white tassels.

Vardy is believed to have posted the picture after Leicester's 2-1 win at Norwich on Saturday afternoon.

The former Fleetwood ace scored the opening goal in the match and now has 120 Premier League goals to his name.
