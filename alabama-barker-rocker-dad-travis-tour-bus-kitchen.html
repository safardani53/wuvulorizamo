ALABAMA Barker has given fans a glimpse of her dad Travis' epic tour bus in a new social media post.

Alabama, 17, took to her Instagram Story late on Thursday evening and shared a video from inside the vehicle.

Set to the backdrop of the track How by Lil Baby, the brief clip gave fans a glimpse of Travis' home away from home featuring a huge brown kitchen with a large sink, a built-in microwave, and plenty of cabinet space.

Alabama started the video by focusing on a huge television screen, which was placed above a comfortable-looking cream leather chair.

There appeared to be several suitcases piled up next to the seat and a bowl of fruit placed next to a coffee machine on the kitchen side.
