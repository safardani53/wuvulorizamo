BILLIE Faiers has been cruelly mum-shamed after she showed off her kids expensive new school shoes.

The ITVBe reality star, 33, said she had finished her school uniform shopping last month ahead of the new term.

She shared a photo of her nine-year-old daughter Nelly and son Arthur, six, in their new PE kit.

Nelly flashed a big smile while wearing her new outfit, comprising of a white t-shirt, a black sports leggings and a pair of Nike socks, while Arthur looked thrilled with similar sportswear.

Billie - who shared the advertisement on her social media - said she was delighted with the Nike footwear from retailers Schuh.

The mum-of-three wrote: "So excited for the summer holidays.
