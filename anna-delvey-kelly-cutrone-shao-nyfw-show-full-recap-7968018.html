Though Kelly Cutrone and Anna Delvey booked space at the restaurant next door for an official afterparty, they chose to stay on the rooftop to hang out with a few people from the press, Shao Yang and some friends. Delvey joked that she didn't really have a choice.

The group chatted with everyone and was overall in good spirits. Delvey came and went a few times — once going to get a La Croix for herself ("pure" flavor, for those wondering) — but was always happy to come back and take pictures with everyone hanging out.

Kelly Cutrone and Anna Delvey brought in the best of the best for designer Shao Yang, who showed her collection under the moniker SHAO on Sept. 11.
