Wayne Brady Will Star As Aaron Burr In Chicago Run Of Hamilton

February 21, 2024
blog

The Let's Make A Deal host will join the Chicago cast of the Tony Award-winning musical, Hamilton.


​History has its eyes on you, Wayne Brady!

The charismatic host of CBS' Let's Make A Deal will join the Chicago cast of Hamilton to play the role of Aaron Burr from Jan. 17, 2017 through Apr. 9.

Hamilton, the widely popular musical centered around the epic feud between Federalist Party founder Alexander Hamilton and Vice President Aaron Burr, received 11 awards at the 70th Annual Tony Awards held in June 2016. 
 
Brady, who regularly belts it out on Let's Make A Deal, is no stranger to live theater and has starred in the stage productions of Kinky Boots, Hair, and Chicago.
