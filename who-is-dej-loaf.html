Who is DeJ Loaf?

Born on April 8, 1991 - DeJ Loaf is an American rapper from Detroit, Michigan.

Real name Deja Trimble, she rose to fame in the 2010s for her well-known hip-hop singles.

Her stage name is a combination of her first name - Deja - and her hobby of collecting Air Jordan sneakers - hence, loafer.

She released her commercial mixtape, Sell Sole, in 2014 and her first studio album - Sell Sole II - in 2020.

Who is DeJ Loaf dating?

From 2014 to 2016, the hip-hop hitmaker was romantically tied to fellow rapper, Lil' Durk.

The two called it quits after two years of dating, but reportedly remain on friendly terms.

Lil' Durk's fiancée, India Royale, even flirted with the idea of her other half and Dej reuniting for a new song.

In October of 2021, India tweeted: "I would not mind if Durk and Dej Loaf made more music together. That was a time. Iykyk"

The rapper replied to her former flame's fiancée: "That's solid."
