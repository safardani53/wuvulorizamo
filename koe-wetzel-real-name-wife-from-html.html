What is Koe Wetzel's Real Name?

Koe Wetzel doesn't seem like a real name for someone born in the south, and that is because it isn’t. Wetzel revealed his full name during a Twitter interaction with his fans in February 2018.

A Twitter user named Samantha Schwenke asked Wetzel what his real name was. Replying to the person’s tweet, he said that his real name was Ropyr Madison Koe. 

Wetzel is the singer's family name, making his full name Ropyr Madison Koe Wetzel. He has also used his full moniker in his Facebook account, which is inactive since 2016.

Where Is Koe Wetzel From?

Wetzel was born on July 14, 1992, in Pittsburg, Texas. The 28-year-old got inclined to country music because of his family, specifically his mother, Julie Wetzel.

Julie is a country music artist who traveled from one town to another for her shows in the Opry circuit — a phrase used to denote the country music concerts held all over Texas. Wherever she performed, Wetzel went with her, being inspired every day. 

While his mother inspired him to take music, his family members contributed to his musical evolution.

It was his grandfather who introduced him to Creedence Clearwater Revival, an American rock band. And, his father got him into the world of hip-hop as he had a connection with rappers like Notorious B.I.G., Tupac, and 50 Cent.

With time, he developed a liking for alternative rock. He listened to Garth Brooks, Johnny Cash, George Jones, Willie Nelson, Smashing Pumpkins, Soundgarden, and Nirvana.
