London rivals Fulham and Arsenal clash in what's become a must-win contest for Mikel Arteta's Arsenal.

Taking one pointy from a possible six has left Arsenal in second position, two points behind leaders Liverpool and three ahead of Manchester City, who have a crucial game in hand.

The Gunners created more than enough opportunities midweek, finishing the defeat with 30 attempts, eight on target and an xG of 2.87.

But a trip to Craven Cottage might just be the best antidote for their recent struggles.

Arsenal have won on their last five visits to Fulham, winning by an aggregate score of 15-2. They have also taken 17 points on the road this season, and will boast the best away record in the division if they claim three points on Sunday.

As for Fulham, they've taken a nosedive since back-to-back 5-0 wins over Nottingham Forest and West Ham.
