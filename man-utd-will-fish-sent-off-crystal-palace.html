MANCHESTER UNITED youngster Will Fish was sent off after "rugby tackling" Crystal Palace star Victor Akinwale in Tuesday's pre-season friendly.

The defender, 19, was a last-minute call-up to Erik ten Hag's squad for their trip to Australia, replacing Axel Tuanzebe.

But he lasted just 19 minutes of Tuesday's match at the Melbourne Cricket Ground after getting himself in a tangle.

Fish came off the bench after 65 minutes to replace captain Harry Maguire, with United well in control at 3-0 up.

But within 20 minutes - and after Palace pulled one back - Fish was shown a straight red.

The youngster desperately attempted a swipe at the ball to clear it as it rolled towards Akinwale.
