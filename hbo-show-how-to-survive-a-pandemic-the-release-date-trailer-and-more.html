HBO Show How To Survive a Pandemic, The Release Date, Trailer And More

How to Survive a Pandemic is a planned HBO original that depicts how humanity prepares for and responds to a pandemic. The program will premiere on March 29 and is directed and co-written by Academy Award-nominated filmmaker David France.

The show gives viewers a behind-the-scenes look at global research, development, and the ultimate distribution of the highly awaited COVID-19 vaccinations in order to end the epidemic for good.

The Tale Behind How To Survive A Pandemic

The program, which began production in early 2020, is regarded as the largest public health initiative in the history of entertainment. The unit worked on the show for the following 18 months, collaborating with international health organizations to give it a correct structure. The show is also expected to shed light on government agencies’ failings.

The presentation, which was produced by Public Square Films in collaboration with Sandbox Films and Impact Partners, was shot on five continents and contained some of the most prominent figures in modern medicine.

Dr. Anthony Fauci, director of the National Institute of Allergy and Infectious Diseases in the United States; Dr. Albert Bourla, CEO and chairman of Pfizer; Dr. Kizzmekia Corbett, co-developer of the Moderna vaccine at the National Institutes of Health in the United States, and others are among them.

Director’s Opinion

Director David France Has The Following To Say About How To SurviveAa Pandemic

“We have failed politically in the field of public health, and if that failure goes ignored, it will go down in history as the core failure of our response…to this menace,” he continued.

“If we don’t address that problem, if we don’t get rid of nationalism and greed, if we don’t get rid of these national borders that surround our reaction to a virus that knows no national borders, we’ll end up with the same result every time.”

What Are The Benefits Of Watching The Show?

In the last 24 months, the globe has been confronted with a peril that threatens humanity’s fundamental survival. The damage was multi-faceted and broad.

In its brief run, How to Survive a Pandemic attempted to showcase the true tale, as well as the efforts and problems faced by the worldwide medical community. The show is absolutely worth seeing to observe how the world works even in the most difficult times.
