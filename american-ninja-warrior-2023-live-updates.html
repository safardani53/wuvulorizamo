MyKayla Skinner competed with her husband

MyKayla Skinner married her longtime boyfriend Jonas Harmer in 2019.

According to his Instagram bio, Harmer "runs a YouTube channel, day trade crypto, and manage a sales team for Vivint Smart Home."

On June 7, 2020, she shared a clip on YouTube teaching Harmer how to do gymnastics and captioned the clip: “So I finally got my husband to admit that gymnastics is hard, it may have taken an entire day of filming but it was worth it. 

“Watch his attempts and try to not laugh.”

Both Skinner and Harmer often post photos of their relationship on social media and give their followers a peek into their life.

MyKayla and Jonas competed during last night's Couples Championship, but didn't make it to the second round.

About MyKayla Skinner

Mykayla Skinner, 26, is an American Olympian.

She was the 2016 Olympic alternate on the Women's Gymnastics team and competed at the 2014 World Championships.

Her participation earned the US team a gold medal, and she also won an individual bronze medal on vault.

Skinner has won ten total medals at the USA Gymnastics National Championships during her senior career.

She lives in Gilbert, Arizona, and has three older siblings, Jeremy, Chelsea, and Katie, two of whom are former gymnasts themselves.
