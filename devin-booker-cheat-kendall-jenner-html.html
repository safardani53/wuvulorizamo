Did Devin Booker Cheat on Kendall Jenner?

June 23, 2024
blog

Did Devin Booker cheat on Kendall Jenner, or has the duo broken up and moved on with new people?

According to the latest DeuxMoi reports, an anonymous tipper from Phoenix, Arizona, had spotted the NBA player with a high-end someone of something that they asked the outlet to redact.

He was seen at the happening pizzeria, Bottled Blonde in Old Town, a couple of times and a few high-end places in downtown Phoenix. Although the user did not reveal who Booker was with, the post’s title was “Dev Hooker,” followed by the question, “Devin having fun?”

Later, the tea page followed up with another blind item that did not mention Booker explicitly, but fans were convinced that it was about him. 

The tipper mentioned that “a certain A list celebrity was spotted with someone other than his very famous reality star girlfriend.” They also noted that the celebrity always hosted pool parties that resembled more of a brothel.

And the worst part of this situation was that the “celebrity” gave out his girlfriend’s brand of drink as party favors. It is worth noting that Jenner has her own tequila brand called 818. 

Redditors Break Down the Cheating Allegations

This blind item made its way to Reddit, where Redditors discussed if Booker really cheated on his girlfriend and the nature of the couple’s relationship.

Many seemed to opine that the duo was probably in a fake PR relationship and that they met up once a week for the paparazzi, and the rest of the time, they did whatever they wanted. 

One user reasoned that Jenner would benefit from the publicity even if the relationship involved cheating or was messy because, according to them, as a top model, it was better for her to be seen as promiscuous rather than sexually undesirable.

Another user agreed it was beneficial for Jenner but gave a different reason. They noted that her relationships would at least stop people from harping on her to get married and have babies because a relationship could mean a marriage would follow soon enough.

A third user noted that all the Kardashian/Jenner sisters collectively concluded that all the men they dated or married would cheat on them. Therefore, they did not consider cheating a deal breaker but a part of being in a relationship. 

Kendall Jenner and Devin Booker's Reconcilation

So far, neither Booker nor Jenner have spoken about their relationship.

On June 22, 2022, E! News reported that the couple had reportedly called it quits after two years of dating.

A source told the outlet they had “hit a rough patch recently and [had] been split for about a week and a half.”

They added that Booker and Jenner enjoyed each other’s company during Kourtney Kardashian and Travis Barker’s wedding in Italy, “but once the duo got back, they felt like they weren’t aligned and realized they have very different lifestyles.”

Less than a week later, the outlet took back their statements and reported that the couple was spotted looking cozy at the SoHo House in Malibu.
