IF you have joined the air fryer gang and want to impress your loved ones this Christmas, we’ve got just the recipe for you.

Food fan Susannah Saville, known on TikTok as @freefromlifestyle, recently took to the video sharing platform to share her simple but delicious sausage roll air fryer recipe.

But Susannah’s sausage rolls aren’t just your standard sausage rolls - these are the ultimate Christmas treat - a pig in blanket sausage roll.

Susannah regularly shares her air fryer and simple gluten and dairy free recipes to the social media platform and her loyal followers were left very impressed with her Christmas treat clip,

She posted the clip with the caption ‘CHRISTMAS PIGS IN BLANKETS SAUSAGES ROLLS with Cranberry Sauce Cooked in Ninja 15-1 Air Fryer.’

She said: “This is my Christmas pigs in blanket, sausage roll.
