MRS Hinch has given the dated and tired-looking bathroom at Hinch Farm a very modern update - and fans are more than impressed.

Last year, the cleanfluencer, real name Sophie Hinchliffe, moved into a stunning five-bedroom Essex farmhouse that she's loved since she was 11 years old.

Over the last few months, the 31-year-old has shown off the grounds of the property - named "Hinch Farm" - and slowly started to reveal some of the interior.

And taking to her Instagram stories, the cleaning whizz has now given Hinchers a tour of her new and improved bathroom.

In a before snap, the bathroom featured a dark wooden floor, plain white tiles and an awkward layout - and was in desperate need of modernisation.

But after employing a design team to get to work on her dream space, Mrs Hinch proudly revealed the impressive results.
