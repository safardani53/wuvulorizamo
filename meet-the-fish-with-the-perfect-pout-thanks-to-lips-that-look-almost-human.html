Meet the fish with the perfect pout thanks to lips that look almost HUMAN

October 08, 2024
blog

PUCKER up!

This fish is causing some-fin of a stir with its juicy pout - because it looks almost identical to human lips.

The Placidochromis Phenochilus Mdoka fish was filmed showing off its luscious lips by amused fans.

Its distinctive lips are used primarily for fighting and picking up objects in the ocean.

The bizarre video was posted to Instagram and has since amassed over 24,000 views.

It shows the fish staring at the camera with its lips puckered before it is seen swimming away.

MOST READ IN NEWS

The fish originated in Lake Malawi and can grow to a maximum size of 10 inches.

Lake Malawi is home to between 500-1000 different species of fish and the majority of these are only found in the lake.

This particular fish was found at Fish Hut of New Jersey aquatic centre in the US.
