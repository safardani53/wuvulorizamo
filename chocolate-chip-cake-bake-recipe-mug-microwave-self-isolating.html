How to make a delicious chocolate chip cake in one minute using a mug and your microwave

February 14, 2024
blog

IF you’re in self-isolation and have been craving something sweet, you can whip up this microwave cake for one in minutes. 

If you’re doing your quarantine solo and don’t want to bake a massive cake or pile of cookies you’ll never finish, then this is the perfect recipe for you.

⚠️ Read our coronavirus live blog for the latest news & updates  

BBC Good Food has shared their simple recipe for a microwave cake online, and it only takes a few minutes to make - and it’s also perfect for anyone who doesn’t have a lot of baking supplies to hand. 

Start by getting your biggest mug and pour four tablespoons of self-raising flour in it, and the same amount of caster sugar. 

You can either leave your cake plain or add two tablespoons of cocoa powder if you have some. 

Crack your egg and add it in to the mug, then mix it all up with a fork - but don’t worry if there’s some dry bits left.

Microwave cake ingredients

  • 4 tbsp self-raising flour
  • 4 tbsp caster sugar
  • 1 medium egg
  • 3 tbsp milk
  • 3 tbsp oil
  • A few drops of vanilla essence

Next add the milk and oil, and if you have vanilla essence pop that it in now as well. 

If you want to jazz your cake up you can also add in chocolate chips, raisins or even nuts if you have any to hand, but don’t worry if you don’t. 

Mix it all up and pop your mug in the middle of your microwave, and cook it on high for between one-and-a-half to two minutes, or until it’s firm to touch.

The only thing left is to grab a spoon and enjoy your mini bake.
