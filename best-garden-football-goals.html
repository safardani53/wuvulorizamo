How big is a football goal?

A professional football goal is eight feet high (2.44m) and eight yards wide (7.32m).

But garden football goals for children are obviously considerably smaller than this.

The most common size for a garden football goal is 6 x 4' but you can also find garden goals in 8 x 5' or 12 x 6' sizes.

What size football goal is best for a garden?

This depends on the size of your garden! And also how much space you want to dedicate to you or your child's favourite hobby.

You should also consider your child's age and ability as older kids can really put some power into their shots and would need something sturdy and of a decent size.

The other factor is whether you want to leave the goal up or take it down and transport it easily to the park as you'll need something lightweight, easy to construct and ideally with some sort of carry case.
