Nikki Phillips And Dane Rumble Welcomed Their Third Child

March 25, 2024
blog

Nikki Phillips already had a picture-perfect family life with her husband Dane Rumble and two children, Jett and Luca. The family of four has now grown to a family of five. On March 16, 2022, Phillips gave birth to her third child, a girl. She just turned to social media to share her joy with the rest of the world.

Nikki Phillips Third Child

Phillips announced the birth of her baby on Instagram on March 21, 2022. For the announcement post, the model shared a couple of cute photos of her kid. The first two photos were of her napping while wearing baby bandanas. The third image showed her standing between her two boys, who were overjoyed to welcome a newborn sister. It might have been the ideal birthday present for Luca, who celebrated his third year around the sun on March 19. People searched for “daughter name” on the internet to find out her name.

“Billie Rose Rumble,” she began the post, revealing her daughter’s name. She then welcomed her little princess into “this crazy world,” saying the whole family was “madly in love” with her. She ended the message with “16.03.22,” the birth date of her baby child. The news post was inundated with congratulations from her fans and celebrity pals.

“Congratulations, darling!” wrote supermodel Megan Gale. Rebecca Judd added her voice, exclaiming, “Awwww! Hello, Billie.” Phillips disclosed her third pregnancy for the first time in November 2021. “THE THIRD AMIGO HAS ARRIVED. We are excited to announce that we have one in the oven “On November 3, 2021, she said in her post while revealing her positive pregnancy test to the camera.

Nikki Phillips Family Of Five

Phillips’ idyllic family life began after a terrifying health crisis. She was diagnosed with cervical cancer in 2009 after a routine pap test revealed a problem. She took a vacation from modeling to heal, and it was only after her rehabilitation that she met her husband, Rumble. It took three years and a loving partner for her to be pronounced cancer-free, but she did so in November 2012. At the time, she voiced her desire to have a family with Rumble. But he didn’t mind if she couldn’t have children.

A year later, in June 2013, the pair were engaged. During their journey to Hong Kong, he proposed. Then there was the wedding. On May 27, 2014, the Sydney socialite married her musical lover. Their wedding was a magnificent event in Bali, attended by her close friends and family. They had attempted to have children but had failed due to her three miscarriages. Then, in 2017, a miracle occurred. That year, she became pregnant with her first child, Jett, and gave birth to him on July 17, 2018. Luca Fox, their second son, was born on March 19, 2019. With two birthdays only days apart, March is going to be a hectic month for the Phillips family.
