HI-CHEW Reduced Sugar candy: Where can I buy it?

April 20, 2024
blog

THOSE looking for candy with less sugar will be pleased to try the new HI-CHEW Reduced Sugar candy.

HI-CHEW is a fruit-flavored candy known for its chewy texture.

Where can I buy it?

The new Reduced Sugar option from HI-CHEW is available now.

Customers will be able to find it in mass retailers across the United States.

HI-CHEW is offering the candy with 30 percent less sugar than the original candy, PR News Wire reports.

The outlet also reports that the new candy may have less sugar but it is still the same taste and texture that fans know and love.

Many retailers across the US will carry the famous candy from Japan.

Some retailers already listing the Reduced Sugar HI-CHEWS include Walmart, Just Asian Food, Etsy, and more.

HI-CHEW Reduced Sugar is currently listed on the Walmart website for $12.99 for the 60-gram pouch.

The candy company offers other flavors in a variety of packaging.

Customers can choose from loose candies in a pouch or in a stick pack.

What flavors is the new candy available in?

HI-CHEW Reduced Sugar is available in two different flavors.

Fans of the chewy candy can choose from Mango and Strawberry flavors.

The new candy is packaged in a pouch with the loose candies individually wrapped.

Both flavors are separated so customers can choose which flavor they want to try from the pouch.

What are the other HI-CHEW flavors?

HI-CHEW's story goes back over 60 years as the predecessor for today's chewy candy was created in 1956.

Now there are over a dozen flavors for customers to choose from.

The first chewlets were available in a single strawberry flavor back in 1956.
