Fans say GMA's Sam Champion is 'aging backwards' after host posts new updates from the studio

WEATHERMAN Sam Champion appears to be aging in reverse, according to some GMA fans.

The award-winning weather anchor at Good Morning America looks dashing in his daily "Good Morning" selfies on Twitter.

Sam frequently posts videos and boomerangs enjoying his morning coffee behind the scenes at the popular show.

But one video of the meteorologist enjoying his morning coffee got quite a reaction.

"Sir! Are you aging backward??" one Twitter user wrote.

Champion replied with a laughing emoji.

"You have Superman's hair. Love it," another chimed on Instagram.

Other Twitter users replied with pictures of their own morning coffees.

Champion's behind-the-scenes videos have given fans an up-close look into the tenured weatherman's life.

His morning posts are normally accompanied by co-anchor Heather O'Rourke.

O'Rourke and Champion have paired for some joyful moments in their morning videos.

Recently, Champion was spotted wearing Harry Potter-style glasses behind the scenes in one of his former live posts.

Earlier this year, Champion competed in Dancing with the Stars.

Sam's involvement in the competition resulted in a semi-apology from him to the random New Yorkers that he “dipped and spun."

“Robin, I need to take a second to apologize to about half a dozen random New Yorkers that I may have grabbed and dipped and spun yesterday on the street,” Champion said with a grin. 

“But I just – I just needed to express it, but I need to say I’m sorry to those.” 

He has since opened up about how anxiety-inducing dancing in front of a television audience can be.

Though he didn't win DWTS, Champion has won Emmy and Peabody awards for his work.

He is best known for his combined 25-year tenure at Good Morning America.

Champion spent time as the managing editor of the Weather Channel.

He now occupies the same weekend weather anchor of GMA he occupied less than a decade ago.
