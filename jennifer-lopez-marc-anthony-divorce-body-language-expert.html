Jennifer, 41, and Marc, 42, revealed their divorce on July 15, but we asked body language expert Sanjay Burman to analyze a picture of them from the Samsung Hope for Children Gala at Cipriani Wall Street on June 7. He says there are many signs that there is trouble in paradise.

“Look at the directions they both look in,” Sanjay says. “Marc is starting at the camera, Jennifer is already looking elsewhere, not on the same page as him. It’s ironic since it would state ‘we are going in different directions.’ She has also turned her torso away from him, while he is almost trying to push himself against her. Look at his smile, and her lack of smile with intensity. At a time her career is taking off, she is in the spotlight, she is looking uncomfortable and annoyed. She also has her hand against his back slightly…almost like a friend or fan. Not the way you would pose next to your husband.”
