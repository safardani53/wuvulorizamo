GEMMA Atkinson has hit back at cruel trolls who said she “dresses like a boy.”

After doing so, a vile comment beneath her post read: “Gemma people are saying or asking this because they just want a reply from you. Don’t be so naive. The clothes are very you. They aren’t great are they.

“They’re boring and even your partner admits he doesn’t like how you dress. I have twenty lumberjack shirts in my cupboard. You have the most amazing figure so why do you dress like a boy?”

The mum-of-two was quick to hit back after receiving the nasty comment, as she penned: “I’m confused as to how you think I should dress like girl? Do I need to be in pink with frills and skirts?
