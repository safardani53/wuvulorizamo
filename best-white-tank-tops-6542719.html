How to Pick the Right White Tank Top

Material

When it comes to picking the right white tank top, it’s all about assessing what works best for your style and wardrobe and then considering details like fabric thickness. Our best overall pick, the Skims Scoop Neck Tank, is made of cotton and spandex, so it's both soft and stretchy.

“When looking for a good white tank top, make sure it is not too thin or cheap looking,” says fashion expert Andréa Bernholtz of Swiminista. Malarkey also recommends paying close attention to fabric quality and style as well as “stretch and fade factors” and strap-thickness.

Bra compatibility

Both Bernholtz and Malarkey recommend thinking about the straps of the tank top and what type of bra you’ll be wearing with the shirt. Many white tank tops require a nude, strapless bra for a seamless fit, but if you opt for a style with thicker straps, you may be able to wear a standard bra with straps.

“The thickness of the tank is worth considering since white can be unintentionally sheer,” says stylist Samantha Brown. “Having the right undergarments like a nude strapless or seamless bra will help with this.”

Cut

There are many cuts and styles of white tank tops: V-neck, cropped, scoop neck, and more. The best cut for you comes down to your preference and how you plan to style it. Celebrity stylist Liv Schreiber recommends looking for a slightly cropped tank top to pair with high-waisted pants and skirts. If you prefer lower-rise pants and want a top that will cover more of your midsection, a longer-cut tank might be what you need.

No matter which white tank top you choose, we're sure you can find something in your closet to pair with it. "The options are endless,” says Malarkey. “They are the perfect piece to wear alone or layered. Toss on a linen shirt, a jean jacket, a leather jacket or even a blazer.”

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How should a white tank top fit?

    Much like the fit of a T-shirt, you might prefer a form-fitting silhouette or a loose, boxy fit. When in doubt, go for a more traditional classic white T-shirt fit.

    “It’s important for the tank top to fit your body, not squeeze you, but to lay on your body,” Gutierrez explains.

  • What are the different types of white tank tops?

    There are scoop neck, V-neck, high neck, cut out, cropped, ribbed, and many more types of tank tops — and there’s likely a place in your wardrobe for all of them, as Brown points out.

    "I recommend having a range of loose-fitting, form-fitting, crew, scoop, and V-neck with various thicknesses in the straps. Having the right fit for different outfits is important.”

  • How much should I spend on a white tank top?

    There are white tank tops available at any price range, from $5 to over $100.Hanes A Tanks are a great, budget-friendly choice, and though they might not keep their shape as well as some more expensive optoins, the Hanes tanks also come in packs of ten so you can always have a fresh one on hand. We love more substantial options, like the Perfect White Tee Blondie Cotton Ribbed Tank because they keep their shape wash after wash, which might make them the more eco-friendly options (and long term, could be a cost-saving investment).
