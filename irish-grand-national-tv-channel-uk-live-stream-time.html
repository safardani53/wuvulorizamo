When is the Irish Grand National and what TV channel is it on in the UK?

June 25, 2024
blog

FAIRYHOUSE hosts the Irish Grand National on the final day of the 2023 Easter Festival.

The Irish Grand National is the richest event on the Emerald Isle's racing calendar.

It's the flagship race on the last day of the three-day festival which begins Saturday, April 8.

A plum field of 30 runners will compete in the Irish Grand National, all hoping to get their hands on the first-place prize of £237,000.

When is the Irish Grand National and what time does it start?

The Irish Grand National takes place on Monday, April 10 - Easter Monday, 2023.

he Irish Grand National is set to commence at 5pm.

What TV channel is the Irish Grand National on in the UK and can I live stream it?

Coverage of the day's racing gets underway on ITV4 from 2:15pm and finishes at 5:30pm.

This includes the Irish supporting card and flat racing from Kempton.

The programme can also be streamed live on ITVX and RacingTV.
