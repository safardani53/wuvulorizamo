JON Hamm is an American actor best known for his role in Mad Men as Don Draper.

Here's all you need to know about the star.

What is Jon Hamm's net worth?

Jon Hamm has an estimated net worth of around $45million according to Celebrity Net Worth.

He earned about $275,000 per Mad Men episode - a total of around $4million per season as Don Draper from 2007 until 2015.

He earned two Golden Globe Awards for Best Actor in a Television Series - Drama.

He also won the Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Lead Actor in a Drama Series.

Apart from Jon's work in Mad Men, he's appeared in other hit series such as Black Mirror, 30 Rock, Parks and Recreation, and Curb Your Enthusiasm.

Hamm also worked as a voice actor narrating documentaries and voicing characters in movies such as Shrek Forever After and Minions.

Is Jon Hamm married?

On February 2023, Jon Hamm got engaged to his girlfriend of two years, actress Anna Osceola.

Though the two deliberately keep off social media, they were photographed multiple times together dating back to 2017.

It wasn't until 2020 that rumors sparked as they were seen out more often including vacationing with one another back in September 2021.

In March 2022, the couple made their red carpet debut at an Oscars afterparty.

During an interview in September 2022 with Howard Stern, Hamm reflected on the future of his relationship with Osceola.

He said: "I'm in a relationship right now and it's comfortable."

He continues to say he's been "really thinking about all that stuff, that's made the relationship that I'm in now even more meaningful and opened up the possibility of things like being married, having kids, defining a new version of happiness, life, wellness."

Hamm and Osceola finally tied the knot on June 24, 2023. According to Hello!, the wedding took place at Anderson Canyon in Big Sur, California.

Before Osceola, Hamm was in a relationship with actress and writer Jennifer Westfeldt from 1997 until 2015.

Hamm has also been linked to producer Lindsay Shookus.

Who is Jon Hamm's wife, Anna Osceola?

Anna Osceola is an actress born on April 8, 1988, in Cambridge, Massachusetts.

After relocating to pursue her acting career, Osceola made her debut as Charlie in Not Another High School Show in 2007.

She then went on to appear in multiple hit series including NCIS, Law and Order: SVU, and Law and Order True Crime.
