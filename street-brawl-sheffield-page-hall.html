Another man is chased down the street by a group armed with broken wooden chairs.

The video of the huge fight took place around 4.10pm yesterdy and was posted on the Sheffield Online Facebook page.

Crowds can be seen fighting and throwing objects near parked cars.

It is not clear what sparked the brawl but cops are investigating.

The brawl comes after a spate of shootings in the Abbeydale Road area.
