Orville Willis Forte IV, known professionally as Will Forte, is an American actor, comedian, writer, producer and voice actor. He is best known for his work in television as a cast member on Saturday Night Live and as the creator and star of the sitcom The Last Man on Earth. After obtaining a history degree at the University of California, Los Angeles and becoming a financial broker, Forte changed his career path to comedy and began taking classes with the improvisational comedy group the Groundlings in Los Angeles.

He attended UCLA as a History major and was in the Lambda Chi Alpha fraternity after being voted ‘Best Personality’ in his high school yearbook. In 1997 he was hired as a writer for the New York City-based Late Show with David Letterman.
