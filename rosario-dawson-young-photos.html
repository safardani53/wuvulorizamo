Rosario Dawson recently became the ‘Star Wars’ universe’s newest star. The actress reprised her role as Ahsoka Tano in a standalone series on Disney+. She first played the iconic character in ‘The Mandalorian’ season 2.

She had her first breakout role in the 1995 film ‘Kids.’ She then went on to star in the 2001 cult classic ‘Josie and the Pussycats.’ Rosario has had countless roles in memorable films like ‘Men In Black II,’ ‘Sin City,’ ‘Rent,’ ‘Seven Pounds,’ and more. In addition to the ‘Star Wars’ universe, Rosario is also a part of the MCU, and in her personal life, she’s a mom of one and set to be a grandma when her first grandchild arrives in 2024.
