Danny Green’s Holocaust Selfie Sparks Outrage

“You know I had to do it one time lol #Holocaust,” Danny captioned his selfie at the Memorial to the Murdered Jews of Europe on Oct. 8.

Whoa. The Holocaust Memorial isn’t somewhere you should be taking random selfies. After posting the photo, Danny received serious reactions on Twitter and Facebook about his selfie.

@DGreen_14 ‘s selfie at the Holocaust Memorial in Berlin w caption ‘LOL’ is disgusting.When did 6 million innocent people dying become funny

— kitty (@katejelaska) October 9, 2014

Bravo, Danny Green, Bravo. The classic holocaust museum selfie in front of some tombs. The caption screams class

— Gerard (@DaNotorious_GIG) October 9, 2014

Danny Green is an idiot.

— RIP Papa (@WhoaDere_Semaj) October 9, 2014

Oh Danny Green, bro, the holocaust isn’t something to make light of. Selfie’s are not appropriate.. 😯

— Billy (@BCasey55) October 9, 2014

Needless to say, people aren’t happy about Danny’s photo. The Guardian reports that he changed the caption to say, “A lot of history here, more than you could imagine…very sad/tragic things happened #holocaust #berlin.” Danny later removed the Facebook post.

After the outrage, Danny took to Twitter to apologize for actions in a series of tweets.

Yes, mistakes do happen

— Danny Green (@DGreen_14) October 8, 2014

I want to sincerely apologize for the insensitivity of my post!

— Danny Green (@DGreen_14) October 8, 2014

I have great respect n understanding for this country’s history n wanted to continue chronicling my experience in Berlin

— Danny Green (@DGreen_14) October 8, 2014

But showed poor judgement…sorry once again

— Danny Green (@DGreen_14) October 8, 2014

We’re glad Danny has taken the time to apologize for his inappropriate post.
