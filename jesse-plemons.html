Jesse Plemons Biography

Jesse Plemons, 34, is an American actor best known for his work in NBC drama series Friday Night Lights and AMC crime drama series Breaking Bad. Likewise, he is also known for El Camino: A Breaking Bad Movie, Black Mirror, The Irishman, and many more. 

Short Biography Of Jesse Plemons   

Jesse Lon Plemons was born on 2nd April 1988 in Dallas, Texas, the United States of America to Jim Bob Plemons and Lisa Beth. He holds an American nationality and has white ethnic descent. 

Likewise, he went and graduated from the Texas Tech University Independent School District. He also went to Mart High School before he received acting jobs more often.

How Much Is His Net Worth?

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Plemons is worth $8 million as of September 2022. He began his acting career at a young age of 10 in 1998. 

Jesse made his film debut as Hobo in the 1998 film Finding North. He then appeared in several films like Varsity Blues, Children on Their Birthdays, Shrink, and others. 

He made his breakthrough as Landry Clarke in the American sports drama series Friday Night Lights. He starred in the show from 2006 to 2011 alongside Kyle Chandler, Connie Britton, Gaius Charles, and Zach Gilford. 

Similarly, in 2012, Jesse was cast as psychopathic villain Todd Alquist in the AMC drama series Breaking Bad. He appeared in the show alongside Bryan Cranston, Anna Gunn, Aaron Paul, Dean Norris, and others. 

For his outstanding work in the series, he won the Screen Actors Guild Award for Outstanding Performance by an Ensemble in a Drama Series.

Relationship Status: Does He Have A Wife?

The 34-year-old actor is yet to be married, however, is currently engaged to his Fargo co-star Kristen Dunst. The couple began dating in 2016 and got engaged a year later in 2017.
