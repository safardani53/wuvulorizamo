Where is former Southern Charm star Landon Clements now?

July 04, 2024
blog

FORMER Southern Charm star Landon Clements walked away from the series in 2017.

The announcement shocked fans, and now they want to know where she is now.

Who is Landon Clements?

Landon Clements is an interior designer from St. Simons Island, Georgia.

A graduate of The College of Charleston, she began her career in the mid-2000s after obtaining a degree in art history and historic preservation.

Over the years, she has worked as an interior designer, real estate advisor, and writer while starring on the Bravo series Southern Charm.

When she isn't in her design studio, she can often be found traveling and posting about her life on Instagram to more than 161,000 followers.

When was Landon Clements on Southern Charm?

Clements joined the cast of Southern Charm back in 2014.

Over the years, she became a fan favorite, which is why viewers were shocked when she announced her exit in 2017.

In a statement, she explained that she would be returning to California to focus on her career in residential real estate.

"It wasn’t really about filming or not filming, it was just what I wanted out of my life," Clements told People in October 2017.

"I love real estate and the hospitality … That’s what I sort of wanted to get back into.

"I know that if I want to get to the next level of things, I really need to get to work and be with people who believe in me, and training with them all summer has helped me so much.

"I’m excited to pursue other things and we have so much fun making the show, but you know, I was just ready to do some other stuff now," she added.

Clements starred alongside Shep Rose, Craig Conover, Kathryn Dennis, Whitney Smith, Cameran Eubanks, and Patricia Altschul, among others.

Where is Landon Clements now?

Clements spent three years as a cast member on Southern Charm but is now solely focused on her career.

After returning to California, where she previously lived with her ex-husband, James Maby, she went back to school to renew her real estate license.

Nowadays, she can be found in Malibu working in residential real estate in a larger market.

"I love Charleston, but it’s a tiny little town, so it’s hard to keep oneself really busy," she told People.

"I just wanted to get back to California and the West Coast."
