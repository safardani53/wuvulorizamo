Lee Carsley could QUIT as England U21 boss despite winning Euros

September 08, 2024
blog

LEE CARSLEY will hold talks with the FA amid fears he could call it quits after taking England to Under-21 Euro glory.

The coach, 49, presided over a first title for the Young Lions in 39 years with a flawless tournament in Georgia.

Carsley, who is on a rolling one-year contract, is believed to be undecided over whether to stay on.

He will speak with FA technical director John McDermott in two weeks when the latter returns from holiday.

Carsley has been linked with the Republic of Ireland post.

Meanwhile, his assistant Joleon Lescott is set to leave the U21 set-up as he wants to pursue a technical director career path rather than coaching.
