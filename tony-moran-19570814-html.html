Anthony "Tony" Moran is a DJ, record producer, mix songwriter, singer and songwriter known for remixing popular songs. In 2007, he reached number one on the US Billboard Hot Dance Club Play chart twice with "Walk Away" with Kristine W and "Keep Your Body Working" with Martha Wash. Moran also has a total of seven winners on the Billboard Dance Club Songs Music Chart in the United States and he recently topped the Billboard charts four times between 2016 and 2017. As a remix producer, Moran has remixed works by famous artists including Michael Jackson, Madonna, George Michael, Mariah Carey, Whitney Houston, Selena Gomez and many others. In December 2016, Billboard ranked him the 92nd most successful dance artist of all time.
